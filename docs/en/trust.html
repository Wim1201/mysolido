Where is your data?

MySolido is local-first: your data lives on your own PC. Optional services run in Europe.

Local (default)

💻 Your PC

All your documents, passwords and data are stored on your own computer. Nothing leaves your PC unless you choose so.

Optional

🇳🇱 Bridge — mijn.host, Netherlands

Read-only copy of your vault on a Dutch server. Secured with HTTPS, bcrypt and firewall. No American company involved.

Optional — AI answers

🇫🇷 Mistral — France (EU)

Small text fragments are sent to Mistral for better AI answers. French company, European servers. Your files stay local.

Optional — AI answers

🇺🇸 Anthropic (Claude) — US

Alternative AI provider. Small text fragments sent to Anthropic. Only if you choose this in settings.

Optional — OCR

🇫🇷 Mistral OCR — France (EU)

Scanned documents are sent to Mistral for text recognition. Full documents go to the cloud. Only if you enable this.

Default — local OCR

💻 Tesseract — local

Text recognition on your own PC. Lower quality, but nothing leaves your machine.

What guarantees do you have?

  • 🔒
    Local-first — Your data does not leave your computer unless you explicitly choose so (Bridge, AI, OCR). Every optional service can be turned off.
  • 📖
    Open source (GPLv3) — The full source code is public. Anyone can verify what the software does. Nobody can create a closed-source fork.
  • 🚫
    No tracking — No cookies, no analytics, no ads, no registration. Not on the website, not in the app.
  • 🔑
    You stay in control — If MySolido shuts down tomorrow, you still have all your files on your PC. The Bridge is a copy, not the source.
  • 🇪🇺
    European infrastructure — Bridge in the Netherlands (mijn.host), AI and OCR via France (Mistral). No mandatory dependency on American services.
  • 📋
    Open standards — Built on W3C Solid Protocol, ODRL 2.2, DPV v2.3, ISO/IEC TS 27560:2023, JSON-LD. No proprietary lock-in.
  • 💰
    The requesting party pays — In the consent system, the party requesting data pays, not the consumer. Your data is never resold (ODRL prohibition).
🔐

How is your data protected?

EncryptionHTTPS via Let's Encrypt on the Bridge. Local data on your PC, protected by your operating system.
AuthenticationPasswords hashed with bcrypt. No plaintext storage.
BridgeRead-only — no uploads or deletions via the internet. Firewall (ports 22, 80, 443 only).
Share linksCryptographically secure tokens. Optional password and expiry date. Watermarks for traceability.
ConsentEvery permission recorded per ISO 27560 + W3C DPV. Revocable with one click.
ODRL policiesMachine-readable usage rules per folder: who may do what, until when, no resale.
Audit logComplete log of all actions: uploads, shares, deletions, searches.
Crash reportsOptional and anonymous. Only version number, OS and error message. No personal data.
📄

Privacy

MySolido does not process personal data by default. The software runs on your own PC. Nothing is sent, nothing stored on external servers, nothing shared with third parties — unless you explicitly choose so.

What happens when you enable optional services:

BridgeYour vault is mirrored to a Dutch server. Only you have access (password). Share link recipients only see the shared file.
Hybrid AISmall text fragments (~2500 words per question) are temporarily sent to the chosen AI provider. Your full files stay local.
Cloud OCRScanned documents are sent to Mistral (EU) for text recognition. The full document goes to the cloud. Do not use for highly sensitive scans.
Crash reportsOpt-in. Only version number, operating system and error message. No names, no files, no IP addresses.
Core principle: Every optional service can be deliberately turned on or off in settings. MySolido gives you the choice — and communicates honestly what does and doesn't leave your machine.
